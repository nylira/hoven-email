HOVEN is a designer, manufacturer and marketer of high-performance products, primarily eyewear and goggles. Born on the beaches of Southern California, Hoven is rooted in the heritage of surfers, snow boarders, skateboarders and moto—but extends far beyond into the fashion lifestyle markets of entertainment and music.

We focus our energy in marketing the best products available, keeping our distribution tight, and telling the HOVEN story through our athletes in grass roots marketing.
